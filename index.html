Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to key institutional questions regarding participation tracks and administrative guidelines

Who should register for this academic symposium?
+

The sessions are strictly tailored for professional biostatisticians, clinical research practitioners, health inspectors, data scientists, epidemiological modeling specialists, and advanced graduate students interested in mathematical biology implementations.

Is there an option to access the tracks virtually?
+

No. To enforce active interactive peer verification networks, abstract visual defenses, and structural computational networking groups, the 2026 conference is completely restricted to physical on-site entry modules inside the UPSS facilities.

How can early-career researchers submit poster abstracts?
+

Abstract templates can be acquired directly via the registration checkout interface. Submissions require a 300-word methodological focus summary and must be logged by August 1, 2026, to clear blind academic peer-review assignments.

Will accredited certificates of completion be issued?
+

Yes. Registered participants who verify their attendance across both academic tracks will be presented with authenticated institutional certificates of attendance stamped by the UP School of Statistics upon valedictory closing cycles.
